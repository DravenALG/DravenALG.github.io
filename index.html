Biography

I am currently a graduate student at Sun Yat-Sen University, following Professor Wei-Shi Zheng. Now I mainly work on computer vision, representation learning and robotics. Previously, I have also participated in research on model compression.

During my undergraduate time in Shandong University, I have worked with Assistant Professor Xin Luo and Professor Xin-Shun Xu. I have researched on information retrieval there and learnt how to do research as a beginner.
