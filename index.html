Biography

I am currently a Research Associate and Incoming PhD in College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University. I am also a member in MMLab@NTU, affiliated with the NTU S-Lab, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy.

I recently earned my M.Eng. degree in Sun Yat-sen University (2022-2025), advised by Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng, where I develop strong research skills and gain valuable research experience, and also enjoy a wonderful and happy time. Additionally, I was a visiting scholar in Macquarie University (2024), advised by Prof. Longbing Cao.

Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Shandong University (2018-2022). At that time, I had my first attempt on research and cultivated the interest in it, advised by Prof. Xin-Shun Xu and Associate Prof. Xin Luo.
