Biography

I am currently a Research Associate and Incoming PhD in College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University. I am also a member in MMLab@NTU, affiliated with the NTU S-Lab, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy.

I recently earned my M.Eng. degree in Sun Yat-sen University (2022-2025), advised by Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng, where I develop strong research skills and a nice taste of it, and also enjoy a wonderful time. Additionally, I was fortunate to be a visiting scholar in Macquarie university (2024), advised by Prof. Longbing Cao.

Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Shandong University (2018-2022). At that time, I had my first attempt on research and cultivated the interest in it, advised by Prof. Xin-Shun Xu and Associate Prof. Xin Luo.

Research Interests

Research is for curiosity and fun.

I am interested in Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Embodied AI, especially in the directions of:

  • self-supervised learning, generative modeling, reinforcement learning.
  • network architecture, model compression.
  • visual generation, language models, understanding and generation.
  • 3D/4D reconstruction, 3D/4D generation.
  • vision-language-action models, world models, robotics manipulation.
