Biography

I'm currently a third-year master student at Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng, where I develop a good research ability and a nice taste of it. Previously, I obtain my B.E. degree in Shandong University. At that time, I had my first attempt on scientific research and cultivate the interest in it, advised by Prof. Xin-Shun Xu and Associate Prof. Xin Luo. I was fortunate to visit Macquarie university, enjoying a happy and fruitful research journey with Prof. Longbing Cao.

Research is for curiosity and fun.

Now I am mainly focusing on embodied AI, specifically on the areas of general robotic grasping and manipulation algorithms. Previously, I also conducted research on model compression and hashing retrieval.
